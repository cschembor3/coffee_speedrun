Coffee Speedrun

Rules

  1. Participant must be seated at their desk at the time of starting the run
  2. Participant may have another person time their run, or they may time it themselves. If timing the run themselves, they must use the timer on this website
  3. Participant must purchase a hand-made beverage - no "ready-to-go" drinks or snacks
  4. Participant may use either the stairs or the elevator, at their preference
  5. Participant must buy their beverage from Booskerdoo
